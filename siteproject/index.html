HEALTH

According to the World Health Organization, health is a state of complete physical, mental and social well-being and not merely the absence of disease or infirmity. On this site you will find exclusive information and activities that will help you develop new healthy lifestyle habits.

As mentioned above, we as humans are biopsychosocial beings, so our total health will depend on these three factors: physical health, emotional health, and social health. On this site you will find information and activities for all of them, since it is essential to find a healthy balance and well-being between these three important aspects.

We invite you to visit the different sections of Physical Health, Emotional Health and Social Health to discover new healthy habits, information, routines, recipes, exercises and tips that will help you feel better about yourself! Ahead!
